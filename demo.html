About Us

Our Story

Synergy Asia WLL is a Qatar-based trading and consulting company connecting ASEAN products, talents, and business solutions with opportunities across the GCC. Founded by an Indonesian diaspora with long-standing experience in the region, the company was created to bridge market access and cultural understanding between ASEAN and the Middle East.


Synergy Asia supports businesses through curated trading, human resources solutions, and strategic consulting services. The company focuses on building long-term partnerships rather than short-term transactions. Its vision is to strengthen sustainable economic and business connections between ASEAN and the GCC.

Founder's Message

Synergy Asia WLL was established with a clear purpose — to strengthen meaningful connections between ASEAN and the GCC through trade, collaboration, and shared growth. Our approach goes beyond transactions. We aim to build long-term partnerships based on trust, professionalism, and mutual benefit.

Our Services

Trading & Consulting

Explore our curated service offerings across trading and consulting.

Trading

Healthy Food & Beverage Products

We source and distribute quality food and beverage products from ASEAN to meet the growing GCC demand for natural ingredients, functional drinks, and healthier snack options. Our curated selection combines traditional ASEAN flavors with modern convenience, catering to Qatar's diverse multinational consumer base.

Trading

Herbal & Wellness Solutions

ASEAN's rich heritage of traditional herbal knowledge offers a wide range of wellness products gaining popularity in GCC markets. From herbal supplements to natural skincare, we bring trusted, quality-controlled products that align with the region's growing interest in holistic wellness and natural remedies.

Trading

Fashion & Lifestyle

Fashion and lifestyle products from ASEAN blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design. Lightweight materials, sustainable fabrics, and culturally inspired aesthetics resonate strongly in warm-climate markets such as the GCC, offering unique value propositions for fashion-conscious consumers.

Trading

Arts & Crafts

Arts and crafts from ASEAN are increasingly appreciated as home decor rather than souvenirs. Handmade items and natural materials contribute to personalized living spaces, aligning with growing interest in unique and meaningful interiors across the GCC region.

Consulting
